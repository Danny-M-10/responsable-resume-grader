LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
========================

The PDF reports now use a fixed company logo for consistency.

To add your logo:
1. Place your logo file as "logo.png" in the project root directory:
   /Users/danny/Documents/Cursor/Projects/crossroads_Candidate_Ranking_Application/logo.png

2. The logo will automatically appear in all PDF report headers (left-justified)

3. Logo specifications:
   - Format: PNG (recommended) or JPG
   - Size: Will be automatically resized to 2.5" width × 1" height
   - Position: Left-justified in header
   - Aspect ratio: Preserved automatically

If logo.png is not found, the header will display "ResponsAble Safety Staffing" as text.
